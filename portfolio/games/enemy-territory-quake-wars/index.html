Profile Stat s

Player profiles give a quick breakdown of the player and his/her play style. It allows the player as well as others to view their favorite weapon and vehicle. It provides the player with a wealth of information about their play style and how much further they have to go to achieve further progress

Achievements

Players love achievements! With this real time updating achievement page players are able to keep track of their achievement progress at a moment's notice. They can see how many kills, saves, or other criteria need to be accomplished before they unlock the next level of achievements.

Weapon Statistics

With these in-depth statistics on weapons players are able to track their skills and their play style. Players can see how long they have used weapons for, how many kills, and how accurate they are with these weapons. These weapon statistics all tie in with the global statistics that automatically rank users and what percentile they fall into.

Player Comparison

This allows players to quickly compare themselves to others. Players are able to compare themselves to a list of people that they have chosen to compare stats with. These people can be on your friends list or a specially designed list of people you want to track statistics against.
