Hack-a-thons

24 Hours of Coding

Every few months, we have a company-wide Agora Hack-a-thon. For 24 hours, everyone can work on whatever they want. There are only two rules: it must be related to Agora, and you have to demo at the end of the day.

Some of our past projects:

  • Hydra iPhone app for one of our titles in development
  • Android app for one of our new web properties
  • Chat service that connects any iOS device to an Xbox 360
  • Chess game in Microsoft XNA framework
  • Hydra theme song, including rapping
  • Server monitoring data visualization tool and demo
