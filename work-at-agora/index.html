Welcome to Troy

Agora is located in downtown Troy, close to several premier Universities – including Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, right up the hill from us. Historic Troy has stately architecture and tons of great places to eat and shop. We are two and a half hours from New York City and three hours from Boston and Montreal, and with the Adirondacks in our backyard, skiing, hiking, boating, biking and other outdoor activities are available year-round.
